Act as a world-class software architect, senior full-stack engineer, AI engineer, database expert, and UI/UX designer.

Build a production-ready AI-powered Job Search and Application Tracking SaaS platform similar to LinkedIn Jobs, Simplify, Teal, and Huntr.

# Main Goal

Create a centralized platform where users can:

* Discover jobs from multiple job portals.
* Track applications.
* Analyze resumes.
* Receive notifications.
* Use AI assistance.
* Get live updates without refreshing the website.

The system should support thousands of users and continuously update job listings.

---

# Automatic Job Synchronization Engine

Implement a distributed background synchronization system.

## Refresh Schedule

* LinkedIn jobs: every 15 minutes.
* Naukri jobs: every 15 minutes.
* Foundit jobs: every 15 minutes.
* Indeed jobs: every 20 minutes.
* Wellfound jobs: every 15 minutes.
* Internshala jobs: every 20 minutes.
* Company career pages: every 30 minutes.
* RSS feeds: every 30 minutes.

The website must automatically update itself without requiring users to refresh the page.

---

# Smart Incremental Updates

Do NOT re-fetch everything.

Instead:

* Fetch only newly posted jobs.
* Detect updated jobs.
* Update changed records.
* Mark expired jobs automatically.
* Remove duplicates.
* Store previous sync timestamps.
* Use caching to reduce API usage.
